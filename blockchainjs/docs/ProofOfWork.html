Class: ProofOfWork

ProofOfWork()

Simple implementation of a proof of work consensus. In PoW, a miner generates a block by solving a mathmatical calculation. It is about creating a hash value with a certain number of zeros at the front. The first miner is rewarded with coins for providing his computing power ("work") to the system.

Example of PoW in real world: Bitcoin, Ethereum
